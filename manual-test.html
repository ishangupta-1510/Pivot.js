Manual Drag and Drop Test

Please open http://localhost:3004 in your browser.

  1. Click on "Sample Data" button to load sample data
  2. Try to drag a field (like Product) to the Rows area
  3. If drag doesn't work, click on the Rows area first, then click on a field
  4. Check the browser console (F12) for any error messages

The console should show:

  • "Drag start: [field_name] from: available" when you start dragging
  • "Drop event on zone: rows" when you drop
  • "Field clicked: [field_name]" when you click a field
